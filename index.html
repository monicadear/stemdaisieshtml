Stem Daisies

Making a positive difference by supporting girls in STEM

About

Upload your video to YouTube

  1. Women in science, technology, engineering, math: Please make a short film (~3 minutes) of yourself - share your experience with your career, in words that a 1st grader would understand
  2. Post to YouTube with hashtag #STEMDaisies
  3. Invite another woman in STEM to do the same.

Why?

We use these videos to:

  • A) normalize the process of asking a question, formulating a hypothesis, researching and collecting data, analyzing results, and reporting on findings.
  • B) expand our girlsâ€™ notions of what jobs are available to them, and what kinds of amazing science is available for them to learn.
  • C) connect with other women and learn/share stories.

More Ideas

If you need some ideas about what to put in your video:

  • Why is your field important?
  • What is your typical day like? Give us a glimpse of your tools, office, instruments, lab, or facility.
  • How did you get involved with your work? Mention your field of study, interest as a child, hobbies, or extracurricular activities.

Background

This project started for a Daisies Girl Scout troop, so the girls could learn how to be involved with STEM at a very early age. Please do submit your video! Thank you for sharing your knowledge with the next generation!
